## Thoughts & Review
* **What I Liked**: The entire world creation. It feels too real, detailed. I like too the life of the protagonist, they must really fight for each thing and don't feel like they have forced antagonist or plot armor or whatever thing like these
* **What I Disliked**: When the dragon comes in on the equation, all the novel goes drop down. The writing was good but appresurated, and the end feels incomplete. I, as reader, waited for more to come but it end, and it give us a finale without a FMC defined and the world was poorly explored on the main novel, only the academy was described. 
* **Key Characters / Arcs**: All the FMC have a main participation, except the princess on the end of the story. All the arks was interesting, but i don't like the last one. 
* **Overall Impressions**: It was a good story, and i understand why the autor want to end the novel: it was their first famous novel and he want to continue with another thing. It desire to read, but it give me with the expectation of more at the end.
